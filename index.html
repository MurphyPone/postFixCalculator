About Postfix:

Postfix notation removes the need for parentheses in order to help the computer understand which operations must be performed before others.

Bad

3 + 5 * 2 = 16

3 + (5 * 2) = 13

Good

3 5 2 * + = 13

5 2 * 3 + = 13
